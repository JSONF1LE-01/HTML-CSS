SONG NAME

Noah Olivier Smith 
            (born February 26, 2000), better known by his stage name Yeat, 
            is an American recording artist, producer, and engineer from Lake Oswego, 
            Oregon and based in Los Angeles, California.

            In an interview with Our Generation Music, Yeat explained the origins of his
             stage name, expressing how he wanted a short name that’s recognizable.
            
                I’m not gon' lie, it was kinda just like some high shit. […] It’s like, 
                uh, I just wanted to do something like one word, I don’t know…
